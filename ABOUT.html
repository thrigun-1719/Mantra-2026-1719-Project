Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate travelers, Bharat Yatra is a homegrown Indian travel company dedicated to showcasing the incredible diversity of our nation.

We believe travel is not just about visiting places — it's about connecting with people, tasting local flavors, and creating stories that stay with you forever. Every journey we craft is designed to offer an authentic slice of India, from the backwaters of Kerala to the peaks of the Himalayas.

With a team of experienced travel experts, we handpick the best destinations, accommodations, and activities to ensure you have a seamless and memorable journey.
